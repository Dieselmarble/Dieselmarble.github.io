Zhongxuan Li

I was born in Shandong, China. I had experience in Huawei Optical Business Product Line, where I focused on robotics, computer vision and network optimisations. Before Joined Huawei, I graduated from Imperial College London with an integrated engineering (MEng) degree (thesis). I had research experience with Dr. Petar Kormushev and Dr. Wei Dai at Imperial. And with Dr. David Sharp from Ocado Technology.

I have a broad interst in various robotic topics. In particular, I work on task and motion planning (TAMP) and learning form demonstrations(LfD). My ultimate goal is to develop robots that can learn, reason, interact and evolve in real-world settings.

I have a great fondness for the arts besides my daily work. I'm deeply passionate about history and philosophy, especially comparative study of Chinese and Western philosophy. I'm also into law & politics.
