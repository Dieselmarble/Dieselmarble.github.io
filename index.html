Research and Projects
 SmartMate: A compact, low-cost mobile manipulator designed for domestic households.
Huawei, 2021  
video / code /

This project designs a mobile manipulator for indoor human environments can serve as versatile devices that perform a variety of tasks. The exterior design consists of a two-wheeled differential drive mobile base, a lift, and a telescoping arm configured to achieve Cartesian motion at the end of the arm. The robot is equipped with stereo cameras for mapping and trajectory planning in 3D, as well as microphone-arry for word reasoning. Semantic-SLAM and a graph based object encoder is used to achieve like-human intelligence.

Lienar Motor: High Dynamic Range View Synthesis from Noisy Raw Images
Ocado Technology, 2018  
poster / video

This novel linear motor is one of the projects during my placement at Ocado Technolgy. I designed and developed this linear motor independently, from mechanical design, finite element analysis, to embedded programming in C. A STM32 microcontroller and six step inverter is used to perform field-oriented control of this BLDC inherent linear motor. Researched in linear PMSM (permanent magnetic synchronous motor), designed the future logistic system equipped with linear motor. Conducted electromagnetic finite element analysis and motor trapezoidal control, implemented the prototype in STM32.
